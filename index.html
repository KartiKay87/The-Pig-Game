Instructions to the Pig Game

  • Try rolling the dice without getting a "1".
  • If "1" appears on the dice, the other player gets the turn.
  • You can hold your current score if you think that you'll get a "1" in the next roll.
  • Everytime the score is held, the turn is passed onto the next player.
  • The first person to score a score of 100 wins.
  • Everytime a new game is started, starting turns are also reversed from the previous game.
  • Last but not the least enjoy playing the pig Game!!!!
